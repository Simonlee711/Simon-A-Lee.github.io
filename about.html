about the lab

Simon A. Lee is a Final Year Ph.D. Candidate at UCLA and located in the Department of Computational Medicine.

His work centers around building new algorithms and approaches for health data and has contributed to literature in fields including artificial intelligence, and digital health.

One of his central goals is to build algorithms or frameworks that are conceptualized as research projects but eventually brought to real users and patients as a means of improving their daily lives or interfacing with their health in easier ways..

He's been fortunate to be supported by the Warren Alpert Foundation as an Ai Fellow, and has been a member of multiple frontier labs including Google Research, Samsung Research among other past affiliations.

Outside of research, he likes to socialize, travel, and experience brand new things. Please do reach out if you have any inquiries regarding both personal and social at simonlee711 [at] g [dot] ucla [dot] edu.
